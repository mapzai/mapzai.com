Thanks for dropping by our blog. We are currently building out the MAPZAI platform and are in pre-beta. MAPZAI is an easy to use and powerful platform for displaying your geospatial data, business data and generating insights and strategy.

Why are you doing this?

We have used various map platforms over the years from basic Google Maps to professional, full-featured GIS systems for professional surveyors and geospatial analysts. What has been lacking is a simple tool to quickly load data and visualise it geographically, analyse relationships and extract insights or visualisations. Mapzai is our attempt at building such a platform.

Why blog?

As users of open-source tools, software and data we are big believers in the open-source movement. Our goals are to allow more people to make use of open data and open source tools to learn, gain insights and understand.

How can I get in touch?

We actively encourage comments on our blog. We expect and kindly request that all comments stay on topic and civilised, but other than that we hope to spark healthy debate.
